Mr Coffee is open all-day Tuesday to Sunday from 6:30am until late.

You don’t need to make a reservation and we reserve space for walk-ins however it is advised if you are visiting for a special occasion.

If you’ve chosen Mr Coffee to celebrate any special occasion then let us know and we can book you a space for an aperitif and a home-made cake for you.

Please provide a contact telephone number or email address when booking in with us.
